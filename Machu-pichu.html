Facts about Machu Picchu
  • Machu Picchu is an ancient Inca city located in the Andes Mountains, at an altitude of 7,970 feet (2,430 meters).
  • It was built in the 15th century and abandoned about 100 years later, likely due to the Spanish Conquest.
  • The site includes agricultural terraces, temples, and palaces, constructed without mortar.
  • Machu Picchu was rediscovered by American explorer Hiram Bingham in 1911.
  • It is believed to have been a royal estate or religious site for Inca leaders.
