Facts about petra
  • Petra is known as the "Rose City" because of the color of the stone from which it is carved.
  • It was the capital of the Nabataean Kingdom around the 4th century BC.
  • Petra features incredible rock-cut architecture, including temples, tombs, and an amphitheater.
  • The city's water conduit system was an engineering marvel, allowing it to thrive in the desert.
  • Petra was rediscovered by the Western world in 1812 by Swiss explorer Johann Ludwig Burckhardt.
