Great wall of China
A massive, ancient wall spanning over 13,000 miles, built to protect China from invasions and raids.
Taj Mahal India
A stunning white marble mausoleum in Agra, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, celebrated for its intricate design and symmetry.
Chichén Itzá Mexico
A large pre-Columbian archaeological site, famous for its step pyramid, El Castillo, and its role in Mayan culture.
Christ the Redeemer Brazil
A towering 98-foot statue of Jesus Christ with outstretched arms, overlooking Rio de Janeiro from Mount Corcovado.
Colosseum Italy
An iconic Roman amphitheater in Rome, once the stage for gladiator battles and public spectacles, now a symbol of ancient Roman engineering.
Machu Picchu Peru
An ancient Incan city set high in the Andes Mountains, renowned for its sophisticated dry-stone construction and breathtaking views.
Petra Jorden
A historical city carved into rose-red cliffs, known for its intricate rock-cut architecture and water conduit system.
