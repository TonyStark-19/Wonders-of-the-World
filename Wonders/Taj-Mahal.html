Facts about Taj Mahal
  • The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.
  • Construction began in 1632 and took 22 years to complete, involving over 20,000 workers.
  • The Taj Mahal is considered a masterpiece of Mughal architecture, blending Islamic, Persian, Ottoman, and Indian styles.
  • The central dome of the Taj Mahal is 240 feet high (73 meters), and the structure is surrounded by gardens.
  • The entire complex includes a mosque, a guest house, and is flanked by four minarets that slightly tilt outward to protect the tomb.
