Facts about Christ the Redeemer
  • Christ the Redeemer is a massive statue of Jesus Christ located in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
  • It stands 98 feet tall (30 meters) on a 26-foot pedestal, with arms stretching 92 feet wide.
  • The statue is made of reinforced concrete and soapstone.
  • Construction took 9 years, from 1922 to 1931, and it was funded primarily by donations from Brazilian Catholics.
  • Christ the Redeemer is a symbol of peace and Christianity, overlooking the city from Mount Corcovado.
